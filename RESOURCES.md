# Learning Resources for AI-Proof Skills

Knowing *what* skills to learn is only half the battle. This section provides actionable books, courses, and practices to help you develop the human-centric skills that AI cannot replace.

## 1. Physical Embodiment & Manual Dexterity

### Magic & Sleight of Hand
- **Book:** *The Tarbell Course in Magic* (The foundational encyclopedia for magicians).
- **Book:** *Modern Coin Magic* by J.B. Bobo.
- **Action:** Join a local chapter of the International Brotherhood of Magicians (IBM) or the Society of American Magicians (SAM).

### Physical Training (Body Work)
- **Book:** *Starting Strength* by Mark Rippetoe (Foundational barbell training).
- **Book:** *Becoming a Supple Leopard* by Kelly Starrett (Understanding the mind-body connection).
- **Action:** Join a local Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), Yoga, or rock-climbing gym. The key is *in-person* physical feedback.

### Crafts & Skilled Trades
- **Resource:** [Skillcat](https://skillcatapp.com/) (Online training for skilled trades like HVAC).
- **Action:** Find a local MakerSpace or take a community college course in woodworking, welding, or ceramics.

---

## 2. Human Social Connection & Leadership

### Active Listening & Socializing
- **Book:** *How to Win Friends and Influence People* by Dale Carnegie (The classic on human interaction).
- **Book:** *Never Split the Difference* by Annie Duke (Decision making and reading people).
- **Action:** Join [Toastmasters International](https://www.toastmasters.org/) to practice public speaking and reading a live audience.

### Leadership & Mentorship
- **Book:** *Leaders Eat Last* by Simon Sinek.
- **Book:** *Radical Candor* by Chris Voss (Negotiation and deep empathy).
- **Action:** Volunteer to mentor junior employees at your workplace or youth in your community.

---

## 3. Intimacy & Relational Connection

### Emotional Attunement
- **Book:** *Mating in Captivity* by Esther Perel (Navigating intimacy and desire).
- **Book:** *The Body Keeps the Score* by Bessel van der Kolk (Understanding trauma and physical presence).
- **Book:** *Hold Me Tight* by Dr. Sue Johnson (Emotionally Focused Therapy).
- **Action:** Practice "device-free" hours with your partner or close friends to build uninterrupted, deep presence.

---

## 4. Judgment, Ethics, & Ambiguity

### Ethical Decision-Making
- **Book:** *Thinking, Fast and Slow* by Daniel Kahneman (Understanding human cognitive biases).
- **Book:** *Justice: What's the Right Thing to Do?* by Michael Sandel.
- **Course:** Harvard's free online course ["Justice"](https://online.learning.harvard.edu/course/justice).

---

## 5. Creativity & Innovation

### Challenging Assumptions
- **Book:** *The Artist's Way* by Julia Cameron (A practical guide to discovering and recovering your creative self).
- **Book:** *Steal Like an Artist* by Austin Kleon.
- **Action:** Practice "Morning Pages" (writing 3 pages of stream-of-consciousness thought every morning) to bypass your internal editor and generate original ideas.
